About Re-Bot

The global waste disposal crisis is a pressing challenge caused by rapid urbanization, population growth, and consumerism. Re-Bot is a next-generation robotic system designed to revolutionize waste management by combining automation, advanced sensors, and financial incentives.

Abstract

Re-Bot is a self-sufficient robotic mechanism that leverages automation, cutting-edge sensors, and a financial reward framework to transform waste management...

Key Features

  • Utilizes moisture, magnetic, and weight sensors for accurate waste segregation.
  • Powers automation through Arduino Nano.
  • Offers monetary rewards via a mobile app.

Claims

  • Achieved 95% segregation accuracy compared to traditional methods.
  • Increased public participation by 60%.

Environmental and Social Impact

  • Reduced methane emissions by 40%.
  • Increased recycling rates from 30% to 78%.
