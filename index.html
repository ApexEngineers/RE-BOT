About Re-Bot

The global waste disposal crisis is a pressing challenge caused by rapid urbanization, population growth, and consumerism. Re-Bot is a next-generation robotic system designed to revolutionize waste management by combining automation, advanced sensors, and financial incentives. By promoting efficient waste segregation and recycling, Re-Bot addresses critical environmental, social, and economic issues.

Abstract

Re-Bot is a self-sufficient robotic mechanism that leverages automation, cutting-edge sensors, and a financial reward framework to transform waste management. By eliminating inefficiencies in waste segregation and recycling, Re-Bot achieves a segregation accuracy of 95% compared to 70% using traditional methods. The mobile app connects the waste handled by users to instant monetary benefits, resulting in a 98% success rate and a 60% rise in public engagement. During its deployment, Re-Bot diverted 65% of waste from landfills, amounting to 4.5 tonnes, and increased recycling rates from 30% to 78%. This innovation contributes to better recycling practices, sustainability, and tangible environmental and economic value.

Key Features

  • Advanced Sensors: Utilizes moisture, magnetic, and weight sensors for accurate waste segregation.
  • Automation: Powered by an Arduino Nano to process sensor data and control waste sorting mechanisms.
  • Incentive System: A mobile app calculates the monetary value of segregated waste and rewards users instantly.
  • User Engagement: Encourages public participation through financial rewards and app-based tracking.

Claims

  • Re-Bot achieved 95% segregation accuracy compared to 70% with traditional methods, making waste segregation highly effective.
  • The incentive-based system increased public participation in recycling by 60%, with 72% of residents signing up on the companion app.
  • Re-Bot limited waste disposal rates to 65%, diverting 4.5 tonnes of landfill materials during its 30-day trial period.
  • The recycling rate in the test community rose from 30% to 78% due to Re-Bot's efficiency and incentivization.

Environmental and Social Impact

Re-Bot has demonstrated remarkable results in its pilot deployment:

  • Segregation Accuracy: Achieved 95% accuracy compared to 70% with traditional methods.
  • Landfill Reduction: Diverted 4.5 tonnes of waste from landfills, reducing methane emissions by 40%.
  • Public Engagement: Increased recycling rates from 30% to 78%, with 72% of households actively using the companion app.
  • Economic Benefits: Created 200 new jobs and boosted recyclers' profitability by 25%.
